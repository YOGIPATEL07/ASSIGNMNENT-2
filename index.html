MARGHERITA



TOPPINGS




The mozzarella cheese used in a Margherita pizza is usually a high-quality, fresh cheese that is sliced and placed on top of the tomato sauce.
The pizza is then finished off with a few fresh basil leaves and a drizzle of olive oil.
Margherita pizza is often considered a true test of a pizzeria's quality, as it relies on the quality of its ingredients rather than the complexity of the toppings.
Margherita pizza is an ideal option for those who prefer a lighter and more traditional pizza. It is also a great option for vegetarians, as it does not contain any meat.
Overall, Margherita pizza is a classic and timeless option that is loved by pizza lovers around the world for its simple yet delicious flavor.

DOUBLE CHEESE



TOPPINGS




Double cheese pizza is a mouth-watering variety of pizza that is beloved by cheese lovers all around the world.
As the name suggests, it contains a generous amount of cheese, more than what is typically found on a regular pizza.
The pizza usually features a crispy crust that is topped with tomato sauce and two types of cheese
Typically mozzarella and cheddar, but sometimes a variety of other cheeses are used as well.
While it may not be the healthiest option, double cheese pizza is undeniably delicious and satisfying, making it a great comfort food for those who love cheese.
It's the perfect choice for anyone looking to indulge in a cheesy, gooey, and delicious meal.

VEG PIZZA



TOPPINGS




Veg pizza, as the name suggests, is a pizza that is made without meat or animal products, making it a great option for vegetarians and vegans.
Instead of meat toppings, veg pizza is typically topped with a variety of vegetables and sometimes
The crust of a veg pizza can be thin or thick, and is usually made from a combination of flour, yeast, water, and salt.
The tomato sauce used on the pizza is usually made with tomatoes, olive oil, and a blend of herbs and spices.
Veg pizzas are often considered a healthier alternative to traditional pizzas, as they are lower in calories, fat, and cholesterol.
They are also a great way to incorporate more vegetables into one's diet.

PEPPERONI



TOPPINGS




Pepperoni pizza is a popular and classic variety of pizza that is loved by many around the world.
It typically consists of a thin or thick crust that is topped with tomato sauce, shredded mozzarella cheese, and slices of pepperoni sausage.
The pepperoni used in the pizza is a type of cured sausage made from beef and pork, which is seasoned with various spices, including paprika, garlic, and fennel seeds.
The slices of pepperoni are often placed in a circular pattern on top of the cheese, and the pizza is then baked in a hot oven until the crust is golden brown.
Overall, pepperoni pizza is a classic and delicious option that is loved by pizza lovers all over the world.
